Sketchbook

Spring 2015

Micron pens, watercolor

When I studied abroad in Denmark I had the opportunity to draw for fun more than I ever had before. I promised myself that I would never use a ruler or erase anything in the entire sketchbook, and I think the results are much more interesting than they would have been otherwise.
