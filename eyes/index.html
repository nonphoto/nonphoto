Eyes Through A Car Window

Fall 2015

120 grayscale prints on matte photo paper

Each print is the result of a Processing sketch iterating on one of twelve original drawings. Step by step, a section of the drawing is cut out and overlayed back onto itself. After thousands of iterations, this produces a blackened image that abstracts the qualities of the original drawing. The images are inspired by a collection of early 20th century photographs held by the Library of Congress.
