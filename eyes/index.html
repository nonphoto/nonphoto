Eyes Through A Car Window

Fall 2015

120 prints on matte photo paper

Each print is the result of a Processing sketch iterating on one of twelve original drawings. Step by step, a section of the drawing is cut out and overlayed back onto itself. In the end, this produces a blackened image that retains the abstract qualities of the original drawing.

The drawings are inspired by a collection of early 20th century photographs held by the Library of Congress, though I won't say which ones.
