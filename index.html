Jonas Luebbers is a programmer and an artist, fascinated by the beauty in computer interfaces, simulations, architecture, exploration, and loud music. He graduated from Williams College in 2016 with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science and Studio Art. Last year he spent the summer programming at Michael McInturf Architects in Cincinnati, Ohio. He is currently an iOS developer at Mindburner in North Adams, Massachusetts, using technology to change the way we memorize abstract information.

My programming projects are heavily influenced my artwork and vice versa, so this site (an unending work in progress) features a selection of both. It is built on Jekyll, a static site generator, with vanilla HTML, CSS, and Javascript. You can look at the source code, but you can't have it. I'm constantly searching for the next fascinating idea to bring to life, so feel free to send me comments, questions, or interesting thoughts by any means you find appropriate.
