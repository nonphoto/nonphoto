Though this piece came together with very little planning, it would be the first of many projects involving a combination of my own drawing and generative art. Each print is the result of a Processing sketch iterating on one of twelve ink drawings. Step by step, a section of the drawing is cut out and overlayed back onto itself. After thousands of iterations, this produces a textured black rectangle that retains some of the qualities of the original drawing. The drawings themselves are inspired by a collection of early 20th century photographs held by the Library of Congress.
