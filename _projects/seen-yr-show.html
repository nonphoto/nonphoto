I designed this series of posters for the 2016 Senior Art Show at Williams College, which I was a part of myself. The name was originally a joke, but things snowballed and "Seen Yr Show" even ended up in the local newspaper. Our class had a very wide range of styles, and good sense of humor. I tried to capture the irreverent quality of our work in fourteen different neon-colored drawings over an extremely plain poster. Everyone made one drawing in under thirty seconds with their off hand, which I incorporated into the poster individually.

A special thanks for Clover Powell, who helped with the design. The show was up in the Williams College Museum of Art between May 13 and June 5 of 2016. I wrote about my piece in another post.
