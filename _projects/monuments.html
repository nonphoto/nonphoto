--- layout: project title: Monuments slug: monuments year: fall 2015 tags: - generative art - drawing description: A series of drawings made from programmatically generated architecture in imaginary landscapes ---

The {{ page.title }} series began with a Processing sketch that I wrote to generate random three-dimensional structures. The scenes that they appear in are entirely imaginary. Normally when I draw I always have full control over the outcome of the final drawing. Offloading a share of that control to a computer creates a similar tension to the one found between nature and architecture. I think it also makes the prints feel a little uncanny.
