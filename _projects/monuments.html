--- layout: project title: Monuments slug: monuments type: generative art year: fall 2015 ---

The Monument drawings began with a Processing sketch I wrote to generate random three-dimensional structures. The scenes that they appear in are entirely imaginary.

Normally when I draw I always have full control over the outcome of the final drawing. Offloading a share of that control to a computer creates a similar tension to the one found between nature and architecture. I think it also makes the prints feel a little uncanny.
